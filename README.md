## Cooking Rice

### Ingredients
- 1 cup rice

### Instructions
1. Measure 1 cup of rice carefully.
2. Rinse the rice in a fine-mesh strainer to remove excess starch.
3. Follow package instructions for water ratio and cooking method (stovetop or rice cooker).

**Tip:** Different rice varieties may require slightly different cooking times and water ratios.
